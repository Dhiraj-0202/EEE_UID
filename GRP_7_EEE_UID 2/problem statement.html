Problem Statement

  • Traditional remotes can be tricky to use, especially for people with physical disabilities..
  • Pressing buttons is harder to understand than using natural hand movements.
  • Wired control systems limit movement, making them less convenient.
  • Existing wireless controllers can be complex and expensive.
  • There is a need for a simple, affordable, and easy-to-use system that allows users to control a car with hand gestures.
